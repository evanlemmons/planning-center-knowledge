# Planning Center Blog: Recent Posts (Aug 2025 – Feb 2026)

Last Updated: 2026-02-11
Source: planningcenter.com/blog, planningcenter.com/feed.xml

Blog posts represent major company initiatives and product launches.

## Jan 2026

- **Your 2025 in Planning Center** (Jan 14) — Annual impact roundup. In 2025: millions of service plans created, 102M+ volunteers scheduled, 600M+ emails sent. Users can view personal stats.

## Dec 2025

- **Tasks Mobile App** (Dec 16) — New standalone iOS/Android app for Planning Center Tasks. Create and manage tasks on-the-go with notifications and ministry context.
- **Multiple Calendars** (Dec 11) — Calendar now supports separate, color-coded calendars per ministry team or campus, while still showing the full church picture.
- **Preschool Grade Options** (Dec 8) — Expanded age grouping options: Infant, Preschool 1-3, Pre-K, Transitional Kindergarten (CA), plus region-specific naming for international churches.
- **Year-End Donation Statements** (Dec 18) — Tips for churches preparing tax-season giving statements.

## Nov 2025

- **Dark Mode** (Nov 3) — System-wide dark mode launched as personal setting. First major update under the unified design system ("Project Phoenix"). Especially useful for tech booth volunteers during services.

## Oct 2025

- **Planning Center Community** (Oct 31) — New community platform for church leaders to connect, share resources (like sample service plans), and learn from each other.
- **AWS Outage Recovery** (Oct 21) — Major AWS outage affected PC and most of the internet. Full recovery confirmed, including processing of queued recurring donations.
- **Gap Alerts in Services** (Oct 9) — Scheduling gap alerts notify coordinators of unfilled volunteer positions proactively, before service dates.
- **AI-Powered Lists (Beta)** (Oct 1) — People product: natural language → list filter conditions. Churches can describe a group ("families who attended 3+ times this quarter") and AI builds the query. Privacy-focused design.

## Sep 2025

- **PicoLabs + NFC Links App** (Sep 25) — Launched PicoLabs, an "innovation playground" for experimental tools. First project: Links, an iOS app for programming NFC tags (~$0.25-0.50/tag) to direct people to church resources.
- **Chat Launch** (Sep 18) — Chat feature for Groups and Services. Real-time messaging with dynamic member management (volunteers auto-added/removed as schedules change).
- **Redesigned Donation Form** (Sep 16) — Single-page, mobile-optimized giving form. Identity verification to prevent duplicates/bots. Promotes ACH ($0.30/transaction) over credit cards.
- **Self-Service Profile Editing** (Sep 3) — Congregants can edit their own custom profile fields in Church Center (baptism status, t-shirt size, etc.) instead of filling out forms.

## Aug 2025

- **Fall Groups Launch Guide** (Aug 12) — 7 features to help launch fall small groups.

## Jul 2025

- **Project Phoenix Design Refresh** (Jul 28) — Unified visual redesign across all products: gradient backgrounds, standardized nav/buttons, improved layouts. Open beta. Functionality unchanged. "All the tools you rely on work the same way they did before this refresh."

## Key Themes

1. **Unified design system (Project Phoenix)** — The biggest visual overhaul in years. Dark mode came shortly after.
2. **Ecosystem-level features expanding** — Tasks mobile app, Chat launch, Home dashboard improvements.
3. **AI entering the product** — AI-powered list building in People (beta).
4. **Innovation experiments (PicoLabs)** — NFC tags as first experiment. Signals willingness to try low-cost hardware integrations.
5. **Self-service for congregants** — Profile editing, improved donation forms, reducing admin burden.
6. **Proactive tooling for staff** — Gap alerts, multiple calendars, task management — helping church staff stay ahead.
