## Frequently asked questions about People
For additional questions, contact [support](https://www.planningcenter.com/support).
### How do I transfer our existing member database to Planning Center?
Export your data from your current database or spreadsheet as a CSV file. Then upload your CSV file into People to import your data and begin organizing your account. For more detailed steps to start the process, [see this guide.](https://pcopeople.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/220345848-Import-a-CSV-File-in-People)
### How can I protect people’s data and privacy?
Ensure that anyone with access to your Planning Center account has _[two-step verification](https://pcoaccounts.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/7338514591771)_ enabled, and assign the appropriate permission level to each user.
### Does People have a mobile app?
Yes! You can use the People mobile app to access dashboards, profiles, lists, forms, and workflows from anywhere. Install the People app on your _[iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/planning-center-people/id1029697457)_ or _[Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ministrycentered.people&hl=en_US&gl=US)_ device and log in with your email and password.
### What customization options are available in People?
You can _[customize the dashboards](https://pcopeople.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/4407176046107)_ in your account to see exactly what you want to at a glance, like giving or attendance trends in smal groups. You can also add _[custom fields](https://pcopeople.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/204263134)_ to your forms to collect the exact information you need from people, like a baptism date or food allergies you want to be aware of.
### How is People different from other church management systems like F1, Tithe.ly, Breeze, CCB/PushPay, and Shelby?
People is completely _free_. You can use it all on it’s own, or combine it with other Planning Center products for a more powerful experience of organizing your ministries and engaging with your church. There are no contracts, and you can cancel anytime.
