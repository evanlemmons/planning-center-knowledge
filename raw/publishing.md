# Publishing

Your church app and web editor. Manage the Church Center experience and make it your own with themes, pages, audio, and video.

## Built-In Pages (from other products)

Choose which features to share on Church Center based on what Planning Center products you use:

- **Donations and pledges:** Give once, set up recurring donations, manage payment methods (via Giving)
- **Simple event signups:** Share event forms, receive payment (via Registrations)
- **Connection + community:** Find groups, chat with members; leaders share resources, take attendance (via Groups)
- **Church event calendar:** Show what's going on, link to event signups (via Calendar)
- **Pre-check for families:** Parents check children in/out from their phone (via Check-Ins)
- **Membership directory:** Private listing of household photos and contact info (via People)
- **Volunteer schedule + chat:** Respond to requests, see calendar, collaborate with team (via Services)

## Custom Pages

Build pages with a simple drag-and-drop editor. No graphic design experience necessary.

- **Pre-made content blocks:** Drag and drop text, layout, and event schedule blocks
- **Images:** Add images to make your site personal
- **Buttons and links:** Navigation buttons and links for easy discovery
- **Color themes:** Select colors to match your church's branding

## Sermon Content

**Livestreaming:** Add links to videos from hosting platforms (YouTube, Vimeo) for people to watch on Church Center. Organize content into series.

**Sermon Notes:** Create interactive sermon notes for people to engage with the message and add their own notes.

**Sermon Audio:** Host audio locally for listening on Church Center. Publish to Apple Podcasts and Spotify from Publishing.

## Analytics

Measure Church Center engagement: page views, click-through rates on events, and signup conversions. Understand what's landing with people and adjust your communications strategy.

## FAQ

**Can I create a branded app logo?**
No, Church Center only displays the Church Center icon in app stores. Many in-app customization options are available.

**Can I use my own domain?**
Not currently. All websites created with Publishing will have .churchcenter in their URLs.

**Can Church Center pages be embedded in our website?**
Yes, most pages can be embedded or linked. Some pages (directory, event signups, event request forms) cannot be embedded.
