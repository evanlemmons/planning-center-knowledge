## Frequently asked questions about Publishing
Have more questions? We’re here to help. [Contact support](https://www.planningcenter.com/support).
### Can I create a branded app logo in the mobile app store?
It isn’t possible to create a branded app logo for Church Center. The Church Center app will only display the Church Center icon in the store. There are, however, many other customization options available for the in-app experience. 
### Can I use my own domain with Publishing?
There’s not currently a way to use a custom, purchased domain with Publishing. All websites created with Publishing will have _.churchcenter_ in their URLs.
### Can Church Center pages/features be embedded into our current website?
Yes! Depending on the page, you can use the provided URL to embed or link to your Church Center pages and share them with your church. Some pages, like the directory, event signups, and event request forms can't be embedded. [Learn more here.](https://pcopublishing.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/25632343062299-Embed-or-link-your-Church-Center-pages#which-pages-can-i-link-to-or-embed--0)
### Is Publishing the same thing as Church Center?
Publishing is the content management system for Church Center. Church Center is the free mobile app and website for people in your church to give, sign up for events, watch sermons, and much more.
