## Frequently asked questions about Calendar
Have more questions? We’re here to help. [Contact support](https://www.planningcenter.com/support).
### Is there a way to import my current calendar to Planning Center Calendar? 
Yes! You can import an iCal file from your Google or Outlook calendar into Planning Center Calendar—that way you don’t have to recreate all of your existing events!
### Can I sync my Google Calendar or iCal with Planning Center Calendar?
Sync your events from Calendar with other calendaring programs using an iCal feed. All edits have to be made from Planning Center Calendar first, then changes will reflect in the other programs.
### Can I embed the calendar on my website to share events publicly?
Post events on your [custom Church Center mobile app and website](https://www.planningcenter.com/church-center), embed them on your website, or create an iCal feed people can subscribe to. 
### What kind of events can I put on Planning Center Calendar?
Create any kind of event in Calendar—whether it’s regular gatherings like Sunday services and weekly small groups, or unique events like weddings and VBS.
