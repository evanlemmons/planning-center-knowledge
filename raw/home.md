## Church Reports Dashboards

Bring tasks, metrics, workflows, and events from across Planning Center into shareable dashboards to keep your ministry teams and leaders on the same page.

**Dashboard components:**
- Metrics for big-picture data
- Product-specific reports
- Note pads for announcements and notes
- Quick links to resources
- Tasks and workflows for follow-up
- Event and team schedules

**Example dashboards by role:** Children's pastor, Worship leader, Facilities manager, Finance admin, Pastor/elder

## Task Management

Get things done with your team on events, services, and projects where you are already doing the work in Planning Center.

- **Connected tasks:** Link back to the event, person, or plan where the task was created
- **Share & assign:** Add people to collaborate on tasks and to-do lists
- **Templates:** Create template lists for regular team events, projects, and more

## Church Team Chat

Leaders can start conversations with their teams and groups from the toolbar anytime, anywhere.

- **Collaborate with teams:** Reach out to entire teams or make threads for scheduled volunteers
- **Connect with groups:** Engage with community groups as you go about your work

## Planning Center Toolbar

Follows you through the system with quick access to your next steps, team conversations, and support.

- **Contact support:** Read how-to articles, watch videos, or email the support team
- **Check notifications:** See updates without switching between email and Planning Center
- **Tasks and chat:** Create and complete tasks, connect with teams as you work

## Integration

Home centralizes information and tasks from other products (Services, Calendar, Giving, Check-Ins, Registrations, Publishing). With every product you use, you can automatically centralize data into Home and take action on messaging, event planning, and ministry management.
