# Planning Center Pricing

## Key Principles

- 30-day free trial (no payment info required)
- Cancel anytime, no contracts or cancellation fees
- Flexible billing: monthly (card only) or 3/6/12-month terms (card or check)
- Soft limits on some products (can occasionally exceed)
- Open API for custom integrations

## Per-Product Pricing

### People
Free (always free, unlimited people and access)

### Calendar
Free for unlimited events. Paid tiers based on number of managed rooms.

### Check-Ins
Free up to 10 daily check-ins. Paid tiers based on peak day check-in volume. Includes unlimited stations and administrators.

### Giving
Free up to 10 monthly donations. Paid tiers based on total monthly donation count.
- Credit/debit card: 2.15% + $0.30 per donation (USA)
- ACH transfers: 0% + $0.30 per transfer (USA)
- Processed by Stripe (no setup fees, monthly minimums, or cancellation fees)

### Groups
Free up to 15 group members. Paid tiers based on active group member count. Members counted once even if in multiple groups. Includes unlimited groups and group events.

### Church Center / Publishing
Free: app setup, home page, engagement analytics. Paid tiers add custom page design, themes, sermon notes, and A/V channels.

### Registrations
Free up to 5 event attendees. Paid tiers based on largest event size. Card and Apple Pay transactions processed by Stripe. Includes unlimited events.

### Services
Free up to 5 team members (100MB storage). Paid tiers based on number of team members with system access. Includes unlimited service types and plans.

### Music Stand
Add-on to Services: $5/mo (up to 50 team members) or $10/mo (up to 150 team members).

## Standard Tier Pricing

Most products follow the same tier structure:

| Tier | Price |
|------|-------|
| Free | $0 |
| Tier 1 | $15/mo |
| Tier 2 | $32/mo |
| Tier 3 | $69/mo |
| Tier 4 | $115/mo |

Volume thresholds vary by product (see individual product pages).
