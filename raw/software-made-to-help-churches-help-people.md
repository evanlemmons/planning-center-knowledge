# Planning Center Overview

Software made to help churches help people. For 19 years, we've been building quality software to help churches stay organized, communicate to teams, and connect with people.

## Platform Architecture

### Unified Platform with Specialized Products
Multiple specialized tools for individual ministries, all sharing one database and working together seamlessly.

### Scalable, A La Carte Pricing
- Choose which products to subscribe to and select plan levels based on needs
- Scale up or down anytime with no fees
- 30-day free trial, no contracts, cancel anytime
- People is always free

## Design Philosophy

### Powerful Yet Approachable
- Coordinate complex tasks with easy-to-learn tools
- Accessible to any skill level (non-technical to tech-savvy)
- Important information quickly available
- Efficient behind-the-scenes tools for staff

### Congregant Experience
- Church Center: simple, engaging mobile app and website for members
- Enables engagement throughout the week
- Public-facing experience separate from admin tools
