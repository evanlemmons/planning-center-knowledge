# Music Stand

Music Stand is a mobile app that helps worship musicians annotate and step through sheet music during practice and performance. It integrates with Planning Center Services to sync worship setlists.

## Key Features

### Sheet Music Management
- Customize each song with annotations, highlights, drawings, and notes
- Rearrange pages to flow in desired order
- Import charts from MultiTracks, SongSelect, or PraiseCharts via Services

### Practice & Rehearsal
- Audio player for listening and practicing specific sections (loop capability)
- Metronome with optional visual beat flash
- Learn music at your own pace before worship

### Live Performance
- Advance pages via swipe, scroll, or foot pedal
- Recommended foot pedals: AirTurn DUO 500 and AirTurn BT500S-2
- Any USB or Bluetooth keyboard-style pedal works (no special software required)

### Team Collaboration
- Multi-device sync: Worship leader's page turns sync across all connected devices
- External display support: Mirror music to TV or monitor for band visibility
- All team members automatically get access once Music Stand is enabled

## Supported Devices

- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- Android (phone and tablet)
- Not compatible with Windows devices

## Workflow

1. Build worship setlist in Planning Center Services
2. Add charts to Services (import from MultiTracks, SongSelect, or PraiseCharts)
3. Access and annotate in Music Stand app
4. Use during practice and performance
