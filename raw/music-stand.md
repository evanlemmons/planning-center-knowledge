## Frequently asked questions about Music Stand
For in person questions, contact [support.](https://www.planningcenter.com/support)
### What devices are compatible with Music Stand?
You can use Music Stand on any iOS or Android device—phone or tablet. Music Stand isn’t compatible with Windows devices. 
### What foot pedal should I use to turn pages and play audio?
Any foot pedal that works like a USB or Bluetooth keyboard and doesn't require special software. We recommend the AirTurn DUO 500 and AirTurn BT500S-2
### How can I add other musicians to Music Stand?
Once Music Stand is enabled for your church, every team member will automatically have access. All they have to do is download the app on their device and sign in using their Services login.
### Can I subscribe to Music Stand alone?
Music Stand is an add-on app to Services, so you need a Services subscription to use it.
### Does Music Stand come with a database of music charts?
Music charts and audio files are only available in Music Stand once you’ve added them to your Services account first. You can directly import music charts from MultiTracks, SongSelect, or PraiseCharts into Services.
