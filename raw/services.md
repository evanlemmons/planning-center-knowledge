# Services

Service planning and volunteer scheduling, for any team. Worship planning, volunteer scheduling, team communication, and rehearsal tools all in one place.

## Worship Planning

Plan every detail for every service. Add worship songs, scheduled volunteers, and special notes so you and your team can be fully prepared.

**Plan Setup:**
- Customize your plan (include only details you need for a smooth service)
- Add service times for each of your services
- Include plan notes (sermon notes, special reminders for volunteers)
- Share your plan (with entire team or provide a public view)

**Service Types:**
Create types for different services (Traditional, Contemporary, Liturgical, Youth). Store plan information and volunteer teams under each type.

**Order of Service:**
Set a detailed, step-by-step flow so the entire team knows what's happening and when.
- Add service items (songs, sermon, announcements, communion, etc.)
- Attach files (songs, media files, documents)
- Color-code items for quick finding
- Rearrange service items by dragging

**Services LIVE:** Show your team what service item is happening in real-time.

**Additional planning features:**
- Series naming: Add series title and artwork to plans
- Plan templates: Create templates for teams and service items for reuse
- Media library: Store images, documents, and videos for future services
- Permission levels: Assign different permissions to staff and volunteers

## Volunteer Scheduling

Schedule people for every ministry need. Plan all upcoming ministries by locking in your volunteers.

**Volunteer Teams:**
Place people on schedule according to their team (Nursery, Hospitality, Ushers, or anything specific to your church).
- Add team leader
- Create custom positions within team
- Assign people to those positions

**Scheduling Features:**
- Plan weeks ahead with Matrix view (view multiple plans at once)
- Schedule faster with templates for regular teams/volunteers
- Auto-schedule teams based on when someone was scheduled last, their preferences, and blockout dates
- Send automatic reminder emails for upcoming commitments

**Volunteer Scheduling Preferences:**
- Blockout dates: Volunteers mark days they're unavailable
- Signup sheets: Open signups for self-scheduling
- Household preferences: Serve with family members or avoid being scheduled simultaneously
- Email My Leader button for help with availability changes

**Mobile Experience:**
Services mobile app for volunteers to respond to serve requests, view service plans, chat with team members, and stay in the loop.

## Rehearsal Tools

**Song Library:**
- View top songs (most popular across all churches over 6-week period)
- Include song arrangements (different versions of each song)
- Add song keys (multiple keys, select what's best for your band)
- Create song tags (filter by fast/slow, communion-only, seasonal, etc.)

**Song Keys:** Transpose any song into the right key for your band. Update chord charts and import audio files to match.

**Song Integrations:** Import lyrics, chord charts, and audio files from RehearsalPack, SongSelect, or PraiseCharts.

**Mobile Media Player:** Musicians can watch videos, listen to entire songs, hear just their parts, or loop specific sections.

**CCLI Reporting:** Report song usage to CCLI automatically.

## FAQ

**Is Services just for worship teams?**
No. Services is for anyone who needs to coordinate volunteers or plan services, regardless of ministry area.

**Do I need a new account if my team already has one?**
No, ask someone on the team to invite you.

**Does Services come with a music bank?**
No, but you can add individual songs. Integrate with RehearsalPack, PraiseCharts, RehearsalMix, or SongSelect to pull audio directly into Services.

**Where can I get chord charts?**
Import from MultiTracks, SongSelect, or PraiseCharts directly into Services. Or add PDFs from your computer to your service plan.

**What devices are compatible?**
iOS or Android (phone or tablet). Not compatible with Windows devices.
