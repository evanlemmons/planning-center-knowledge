# Church Center

Connect people with the life of your church. A community-centered mobile app and website, powered by Planning Center products, made for your church.

## Overview

Church Center is the public-facing app and website that your congregation uses to interact with your church. It's powered by the Planning Center products you subscribe to. Manage all built-in features from Planning Center and create a home landing page for free in Publishing. Upgrade Publishing to customize further with your own content (video, audio, sermon notes, custom pages).

Available on iOS and Android.

## What People Can Do in Church Center

**Give, pledge, and manage donations** (powered by Giving)
Give with debit/credit card, Apple Pay, or ACH bank transfer. View statements and manage recurring donations from their profile.

**Connect with community and chat** (powered by Groups)
Find groups by stage of life, day of the week, location, or confidential groups for recovery/grief/support. Leaders share resources, take attendance, and start conversations.

**Sign up for events** (powered by Registrations)
See upcoming events on the calendar, fill out signups, and pay right from the app.

**Manage volunteer schedules** (powered by Services)
Respond to scheduling requests, check upcoming schedule, and connect with teams via chat.

**Check family in and out** (powered by Check-Ins)
Parents pre-select children to check in before arriving. Scan security code on phone to check in/out.

**Update contact info and directory listing** (powered by People)
Access each other's approved contact information and family photos. Edit profiles, household details, and privacy settings.

**Livestream, listen to audio, and take sermon notes** (powered by Publishing)
Take notes during the sermon, join live from home, or watch/listen to recordings on their own time.
